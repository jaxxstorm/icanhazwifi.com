About This Site

This website serves as a captive portal detection endpoint. Many devices check specific URLs to determine if they're behind a captive portal that requires login.

Cache Policy: All content is served with no-cache headers to ensure devices always receive fresh content and properly detect network changes.

DNS TTL: DNS records are configured with short time-to-live values, allowing rapid propagation of changes across the internet.

This ensures reliable captive portal detection across Android, iOS, Windows, and other operating systems.
